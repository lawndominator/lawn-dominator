🌿 Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Summary: Lawn Dominator is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect only the minimum data needed to provide lawn care recommendations, we never sell your data, and we do not require you to create an account.

About Lawn Dominator

Lawn Dominator is a lawn care app for homeowners that provides personalized grass care guidance based on your location and grass type. Our goal is to help you grow a healthier lawn without unnecessary data collection.

Information We Collect

Location Data

Lawn Dominator requests access to your device's location solely to determine your USDA Plant Hardiness Zone. This zone information is used to provide accurate, region-appropriate lawn care recommendations (e.g., watering schedules, fertilization timing, frost dates).

  • Location is used only to identify your hardiness zone at the time of request.
  • We do not continuously track or log your location.
  • Your precise GPS coordinates are not stored on our servers.
  • Location access is requested with your explicit permission and can be revoked at any time in your device settings.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not require you to create an account or register.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information unless you contact us directly.
  • We do not track your activity across other apps or websites.
  • We do not collect payment information.
  • We do not use advertising networks or tracking pixels.

Data Sharing & Sale

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to any third party. Ever.

We do not share your information with advertisers, data brokers, or analytics companies. Any service providers we use are strictly limited to helping the app function (e.g., weather or zone lookup APIs) and are contractually prohibited from using your data for any other purpose.

Data Retention

Since we do not store personal data on our servers, there is nothing to delete. App preferences are stored locally on your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Lawn Dominator is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above and post the revised policy here. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
